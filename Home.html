Examination Details For Leading SAFe Certification

Exam Name: SAFe Agilist 
Certification

Exam Duration: 90 minutes

Total Questions: 45

Test Format: Multiple Choice Questions

Passing score: 77% (Must give 35 correct answers out of 45)

Exam Delivery: Web-based, closed-book, no outside assistance.

Access: Attendees get access to attend the exam in the SAFe global platform after completion of the Leading SAFe Certification training.

Exam Fee: The course fee includes the exam fee.

Certificate Validity: One Year

Renewal Fee: $100 (Annual Certificate Renewal Cost)

Retake policy: After the first attempt, you need to pay $50 for each retake. You can go for a second attempt immediately after the first attempt. For the third attempt, you need to wait for 10 days, and for the fourth attempt, the waiting time would be 30 days.
